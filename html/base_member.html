Unary Operators

For op equal to + , - the following operation must be supported:

z = op x

Assignment Operators

For op equal to = , += , -= , *= , and /= the following operation must be supported:

z op x

Binary Operators

For op equal to + , - , * , and / the following operation must be supported:

z = x op y

Bool Operators

For op equal to == , != , <= , the following operation must be supported:

b = x op y
