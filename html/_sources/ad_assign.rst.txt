AD Assignment Operator
######################

Syntax
******
*y* = *x*

Purpose
*******
Assigns the value in *x* to the object *y* .
In either case,

x
*
The argument *x* has prototype

   ``const`` *Type* & *x*

where *Type* is
``VecAD<`` *Base* >:: ``reference`` ,
``AD<`` *Base* > ,
*Base* ,
or any type that has an implicit constructor of the form
*Base* ( *x* ) .

y
*
The target *y* has prototype

   ``AD<`` *Base* > *y*
