Syntax

cpp_graph graph_obj
graph_obj . initialize ()

function_name

function_name is initialized to the empty string.

n_dynamic_ind

n_dynamic_ind is initialized as zero.

n_variable_ind

n_variable_ind is initialized as zero.

constant_vec

constant_vec is initialized as empty.

operator_vec

operator_vec is initialized as empty.

operator_arg

operator_arg is initialized as empty.

dependent_vec

dependent_vec is initialized as empty.

Parallel Mode

The first use of the cpp_graph constructor cannot be in parallel execution mode.
